2nd THING TO KNOW :-JEEWAN ME KYA HOGA MERE SAATHü§î(EXAM PATTERN)

There will be 2 exams :- Aptitude Test(75% weightage) & personal interview(25% weightage).

Aptitude Test

Time : 180 minutes
Medium : English or Hindi

  1. Stream SA : Total 80 Questions
    Questions will be divide in two parts and further each part will be divide into 4 sections.
    sections will be:-MATHS,PHYSICS,CHEMISTRY & BIOLOGY.
    1st part:- 15 Questions of 1 mark each in each subject.
    2nd part:- 5 questions of 2 marks each in each subject.

    NOTE

    :-each and every question is compulsory (haan haan bio liye ho phir v maths banao and vice versa)üòÖ.
    :-there is NEGATIVE marking of 0.25 in Part I and 0.50 in Part II(tukka nahi marna hai)ü§ê.

  2. Stream SB/SX : Total 45+20 Questions
    Questions will be divide in two parts and further each part will be divide into 4 sections.
    sections will be:-MATHS,PHYSICS,CHEMISTRY & BIOLOGY.
    1st part:- 15 Questions of 1 mark each in each subject.
    2nd part:- 10 questions of 2 marks each in each subject.

    NOTE

    :-Candidates have to answer only any THREE subjects in Part I and any TWO subjects in Part II.
    (4 me se koi 3 he subject 1 number wale question me aur 4 me se bus 2 subject 2 number wale question me)ü§Ø
    (agar jada chul machi ho toh(strictly not advicable))üòà
    :- In case candidate attempts more sections, best of three in Part I and best of two in Part II will be considered.
